FROGGER
An arcade game that debuted in 1981 by Konami. Objective of the game is to guide frogs to their homes across hazardous terrains of car-filled roads and rapid rivers. The game is highly regarded as a classic from the golden age of video arcade games.

Player Controls:
⇒ A - Move Player Left ⇒ W - Move Player Forward
⇒ D - Move Player Right ⇒ S - Move Player Backward
